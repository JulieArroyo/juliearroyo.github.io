About

Designing where brand story meets everyday usability.

I’m Julie Arroyo — a Senior Digital Product Designer based in Chicago. I craft brand-led, story-driven interfaces for broadcast, lifestyle, and product teams, blending emotion, clarity, and craft to make digital experiences feel human.

Over the last decade I’ve worked across UX, UI, and visual systems — from national television websites and cross-platform design systems to small-business sites with SEO foundations and tangible merchandise like apparel and collectibles.

My approach is equal parts structure and storytelling: clear information architecture, accessible components, and a visual language that respects the brand’s voice.
